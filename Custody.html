Buy Bitcoin

When buying bitcoin through Coinbase, you need to choose how you will pay for it. Your choice of payment method will affect the amount you can buy and how fast you will receive the currency. This was a prototype I created to make this information easily understandable.

Choosing a payment method

Your choice of payment method affects the time it takes to receive the bitcoin and the limit of how much you can buy.

Delivery time

When buying Bitcoin with your bank account the bitcoin will usually take 2-3 days to arrive. Other payment methods usually allow you to receive your bitcoin instantly. In my design user can see the different delivery times easily in the payment method table.

Limits

Because of fraud detection reasons, buying with bank account usually allows you to buy the biggest amount. Other payment methods will usually have significantly lower limits. In my design the list of payment methods automatically filters based on the amount you type in.

Try it out

Using HTML, CSS and Javascript (+ jQuery) I created an interactive prototype to display the main interactions of the design. Try typing different amounts to the input field on the left to see how it works.
