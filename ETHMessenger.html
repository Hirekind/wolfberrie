Anonymous messaging for traders to any ETHAddress

You use messaging for most of your daily communication, but when you travel you can't always easily contact your hotel. What if you could message the front desk straight from your phone? In this project I created a mobile app concept that will take the hotel's customer experience to the next level.

Main features

The user would be able to access all different hotels from one app. They would be able to message the hotel, see hotel information and order room service.

Messaging

The customer is able to message the front desk to ask questions or request help. The threshold of contacting the hotel is lower when it only takes couple of seconds to send a message.

Hotel Guide

The customer can see all the important hotel information from one place. The hotel can update information easily to all customers as practices evolve.

Room service

The room service is available through the app. With just one click the customer can order food, drinks and services straight to their room.

Customer view

It was important that the customer view would be as simple as possible. The main actions are easily accessible and the most important information is displayed clearly. The hotel can define their own context dependent notification to show to the customer. Also the address of the hotel is displayed prominently.

Hotel view

The hotel staff is able to see all the customer communication in one easy view. The UI prioritizes messages based on time of arrival so that no message will be left unanswered for too long.
